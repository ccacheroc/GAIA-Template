# Project Identity: Gaia Quiz Management

## Core Identity
- **Project Name**: Gaia Quiz Management (Gaia-PruebaCris)
- **Goal**: A high-premium platform for creating, managing, and taking AI-powered quizzes.
- **Target Audience**: Teachers, Students, and IA enthusiasts.

## Brand Guidelines
- **Visual Style**: Vibrant, premium, modern (Glassmorphism, gradients).
- **Primary Color/Theme**: Blue/Indigo gradients (`oklch` based).
- **Tone of Voice**: Professional yet engaging, helpful.

## Environment & Logistics
- **Local Dev Port (Frontend)**: 5188 (Avoids collisions with other GAIA projects)
- **API Base URL**: http://localhost:8000/api/v1

## Key Differentiators
- Strict Hexagonal Architecture.
- TDD/BDD mandated for all features.
- High accessibility and premium UX.
